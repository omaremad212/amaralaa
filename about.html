My Story

Passion for
Every Frame

I'm Ammar Alaa, a motion designer and video editor based in Egypt. My journey started with a fascination for how visuals can communicate emotion — how a single frame, a well-timed cut, or a smooth animation can completely transform how a message is received.

Over the years I've worked on brand identity films, social media content, event highlight reels, and full promotional campaigns — always pushing to deliver work that stands out and resonates.

I believe great visual work isn't just about technical skill — it's about understanding the story behind the project and finding the most impactful way to tell it.
